## MULCH

**Estimated Cost: $0 (FREE - using on-site pine straw)**

| Item | Quantity | Source | Cost | Notes |
|------|----------|--------|------|-------|
| Pine straw | As needed | Your property | $0 | 2-3" layer on all beds |

**PINE STRAW NOTES**:
- ✓ Free resource on your property
- ✓ Excellent for tomatoes, peppers, cucumbers, squash (acid-tolerant crops)
- ✓ Good weed suppression, doesn't compact
- ✓ Slow to decompose (lasts longer than grass clippings)
- ⚠ Slightly acidifies soil over time (minor - your compost amendments will buffer this)
- Apply 2-3" layer, keep a few inches away from plant stems

**ADDITIONAL FREE ALTERNATIVES** (if you need more):
- Grass clippings (your lawn or neighbors) - good for beans, peas, greens
- Shredded leaves (Durham municipal leaf collection program)
- Cardboard (grocery stores give away free) - good for path suppression
